Experiencing Life at Oak Creek

Throughout our years in the building industry, our team at Trophy Signature Homes has successfully conceptualized and created dozens of highly sought-after new home communities throughout the Greater Dallas-Fort Worth area—and one of our newest communities at Oak Creek expertly captures everything that potential homeowners are looking for in their new home location. 

Settled within the city of Forney, we are incredibly excited for potential buyers to explore everything Oak Creek has to offer—and one of the best ways to see what your life could be like within this new home community is by touring our stunning model home.  

Model Home highlight

Within this new home community, we offer 8 exceptional floor plan designs for homeowners to choose from, and our model home features The Oak II for potential buyers to explore. 

As with all Trophy Signature Home designs, as soon as you see the home for the first time, the modern exterior silhouette will quickly grab your attention. Walking through the foyer will lead you past the powder room and the private dining area before taking you into the gourmet kitchen where you will see the stone countertops, premier appliances and a large center island—perfect for prep work. The spacious family room is just off of the kitchen with the primary suite making up the rest of the first floor—along with its own luxurious primary bathroom and walk-in closet. As you make your way upstairs, the floor plan boasts 3 additional bedrooms, an additional bathroom and a large loft space that could easily become the ultimate game room, home movie theater or even a designated homework space if you have school-aged children. 

The city of Forney

Forney, Texas is a vibrant and growing city located just east of the bustling streets of downtown Dallas. Known for its friendly community, Forney is an excellent place to call home. 

While the quick commute to Dallas is especially helpful for any working professionals throughout the area—Forney also has a number of employment opportunities to explore, including the City of Forney and the Forney Economic Development Corporation. The Forney Independent School District also offers plenty of job opportunities for residents as well as a highly-rated educational system for all school-aged children. 

The city is also home to a number of excellent restaurants, cafes and local businesses that offer a variety of unique products and services. For the outdoor enthusiast, the city of Forney also offers a wide range of recreational activities for residents to enjoy. The city boasts multiple parks and sports fields and just a few minutes away from Oak Creek—and with 22,000 acres to explore—Lake Ray Hubbard offers the ideal destination for boating, fishing or water skiing on a perfect sunny day.
